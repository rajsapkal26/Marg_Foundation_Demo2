what we do for the Healthcare!

The Marg Foundation is dedicated to improving healthcare access and quality for marginalized communities. We believe everyone deserves the opportunity to live a healthy life, regardless of their background or circumstances.

We establish medical outposts in underserved areas, providing essential medical services like checkups, vaccinations, and basic treatment to those who might not otherwise have access.
